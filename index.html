About Me

I am a software engineer located in New York City with experience in full-stack development. I am a graduate of New York University’s Tandon School of Engineering where I majored in Computer Science and minored in Cyber Security. Currently, I am working as a software engineer at Goldman Sachs. I am experienced in a variety of programming languages and tools. I love learning new things and solving complex problems. Experience

I started my software engineering journey during my sophomore year in college as a Networks Engineering Analyst at Deloitte. At Deloitte, I developed, tested, and managed Python programs to automate network infrastructure hardware. My next experience was working at JP Morgan Chase & Co. as a software engineer. There I worked on security controls for entitlement management using attribute-based access control. I also designed a policy decision point microservice to manage entitlements and showcased a live example in a proof of concept user interface utilizing Python, XACML, and Flask. Upon graduation, I joined Goldman Sachs as a software engineer on the Alternative Investments and Manager Selection team. Currently, I primarily work on a Java backend and Angular (TypeScript) front end.
